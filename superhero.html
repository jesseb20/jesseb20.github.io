Spider-Man

Cover to Spider-Man #1 by Todd McFarlene

Spider-Man was the first superhero that Jesse developed an interest in and that interest became a passion as he read the comics through the years. Spider-Man is unique in that his heroics come at personal cost and his problems are real life ones like paying rent and finding work.

Nightwing

Cover of Nightwing vol. 3 #118 by Bruno Redondo

Nightwing is the original robin who grew up and became his own hero outside of Batman. He is known as the "heart of the DC Universe" and is widely considered as trustworthy as Superman by the heroes of his world.
